Frequently asked Questions

Who are we?

Adbond is the first and leading "agro-to-home" development company for both agricultural and home purposes.

Who can buy our products?

Anyone can get our products - individuals, Undergraduates, Corps members, Pensioners, Business associates, Co-operative societies and lots more.

Where are Adbond lands laocated?

We currently have lands within the following local government areas:

  • Obafemi-Owode LGA, Ogun State
  • Ogunmakin, Ajebo, Ogun State
  • Lagelu LGA, Ibadan, Oyo State
  • Majoda, Epe, Lagos State

Are site tours free of charge?

Pre-registration for site tours requires a non-refundable deposit of N5,000.00 foro visitors who do not purchase a property upon completion of the tour. However, visitors who become customers through property purchases receive a refund.

We are an “agro-to-home” development company. We help you secure a future space you can call home.

What "agro-to-home" plans are available?

We currently offer different packages for land, agro and home. Contact our sales team via support@adbondharvestandhomes.com for more information on the packages available for different products.

Why Adbond Harvest and Homes

At Adbond Harvest and Homes, we have aligned our "agro-to-home" products with Sustainable Development Goals 1 and 2 - to end poverty and hunger in all its forms through sustainable agriculture across the globe. As part of our process, we enable enable individuals and businesses to invest in lands, cultivate the lands with the farming options of their choice, and then build structures.
