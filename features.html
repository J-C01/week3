Land purchase

Our Philosophy is to promote wealth, and unity through “agro-to-home” development projects in Africa We encourage our target market to make land investments. Our land subscription packages at Adbond have been expanded to allow individuals and corporations to make outright or instalment purchases.

Agricultural engagement

Once lands are bought from our sales team, we offer agricultural engagement plans for clients to select preferred options and farming systems. We then hire farming experts based on clients’ approval and start cultivating in-bond lands. Annually, our clients earn income from farms engaged in agriculture.

Home development

While clients gain from farm proceedings over a set length of time, we are there for them every step of the way, advising them on structures that can be constructed on cultivated land once it has generated returns.

Why Adbond Harvest and Homes

At Adbond Harvest and Homes, we have aligned our "agro-to-home" products with Sustainable Development Goals 1 and 2 - to end poverty and hunger in all its forms through sustainable agriculture across the globe. As part of our process, we enable enable individuals and businesses to invest in lands, cultivate the lands with the farming options of their choice, and then build structures.
